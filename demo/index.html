The new novel from Isa Baranowski.

Enter the world of POLTER, the new novel from Isa Baranowski.

Lani wants to escape the intense nightmares and dysfunctional life she leads with her single mom in the eerie safehouse that's supposed to protect them from her father. But when a doppelganger named Polter appears one night with the offer to take away Lani's problems, and entire identity, will it be everything she wanted?
