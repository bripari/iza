About Iza

Iza Baranowski was born in Australia and raised by her Polish mom. She lives in New York, and Polter is her first YA novel series.

As a child, Iza was already a storyteller. She grew up creating worlds in Polish for her sisters at home and English for her classmates at school. She later completed her Associate’s Degree in Writing at Melbourne’s RMIT University and a Bachelor's in Creative Writing at Deakin University.

In between working on creative nonfiction essays and the Polter book series, Iza edited manuscripts at an independent publisher in Brooklyn and has been active in the New York creative writing scene.
