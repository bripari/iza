POLTER

& THE OTHER SIDE

Enter the world of POLTER, the new novel from Iza Baranowski.

Lani wants to escape the dysfunctional life she leads with her mom. The intense nightmares and recent move to an eerie safehouse that’s supposed to protect them from her estranged father prove to be too much. But when a doppelganger named Polter appears one night with the offer to take away Lani’s problems and entire identity, will it be everything she wanted?

Before she can decide, Lani will have to confront old fears, figure out what her strange neighbor Magda wants, and discover there are things called “portals” that can take her to a place called The Other Side.
